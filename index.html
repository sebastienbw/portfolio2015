BackPocket

Camp Games Reference App

BackPocket allows camp leaders to quickly find games to play with their groups. Efficient filtering means they'll always find the right game for any occasion. Users can also submit their own games to expand the database.

In order to better understand the needs of the target group, we performed workshops with the camp staff. From there, the required features, user experience and content structure was determined

The end result was a simple, focused application specially tailored for camp leaders.

Sloops!

Naval Skirmish Board Game

Sloops is a naval skirmish game combining elements of worker placement, deck building and strategic maneuvering.

Players attempt to shoot other ships by playing cards to move around the board and perform actions. Sloops is the product of extensive user testing and refinement to balance the mechanics and ease learnability.
